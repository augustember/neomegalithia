Actions

Actions are things your character does. And in the world of Megalithia, your character can do a lot.

Actions Per Round

During fast times, you are allowed a certain number of different action types per round. You start with 2 standard actions and 1 extra action per round of fast times. This may be increased upon gaining certain levels or taking certain traits.

Standard Actions

Standard actions are an action type which take a bit more focus to use. These are most skills, abilities, and increments of your movement speed.

Examples of standard actions include but are not limited to:

  • A skill check
  • An attack ability
  • Most abilities

    • Extra Actions

    Extra actions are actions which can be done briefly and don’t require as much focus.

    Some examples of extra actions include:

  • Giving a verbal command to an ally
  • Switching out a piece of equipment
  • Using a single-use item
  • Throwing an item to an ally
  • Interacting with an object
  • Readying your Weapon
  • Certain abilities

    • Most of your skills and abilities use up 1 of your standard actions for your action allowance

    Decisive and Response Actions

    Decisive Actions are actions that must be performed on your turn during fast times. Response Actions are actions that are done in response to something outside your turn.

    For each additional standard action you take in a round of Fast Times beyond the first, you take one penalty to your roll.
