Armor Traits

If it wasn’t obvious, the benefits armor gives only apply while wearing that piece of equipment.

Heavy

This reduces your movement speed by 5 feet.

Torso

This piece of equipment uses a torso slot.

Held

This piece of equipment takes up a hand slot.

Strapped

This is strapped to your arm. You may not use more than one of these.
