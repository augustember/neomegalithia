Basic Traits

Charming

You gain a +X Advantage Bonus on your charm rolls and +X to your Charm Threshold, where X is equal to your Big Bonus.

Wise

Your Wisdom rolls gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Big Bonus.

Medic

Your Medical rolls gain +X Advantage Bonus equal and a +X Medical Threshold increase, where X is an amount equal to our Big Bonus.

Agile

Your Agility rolls gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Big Bonus.

Aware

Your Detect rolls gain +X Advantage Bonus equal and a +X Detect Threshold increase, where X is an amount equal to our Big Bonus.

Strong

Your strength rolls gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Big Bonus.

Stealthy

Your Stealth rolls gain +X Advantage Bonus equal and a +X Stealth Threshold increase, where X is an amount equal to our Big Bonus.

Tech Savvy

Your Tech rolls gain +X Advantage Bonus equal and a +X Tech Threshold increase, where X is an amount equal to our Big Bonus.

Inspector

Your Inspect rolls gain +X Advantage Bonus equal and a +X Inspector Threshold increase, where X is an amount equal to our Big Bonus.

Fast

Your movement speed increases by 10.

Survivalist

Your Survival rolls gain +X Advantage Bonus equal and a +X Survival Threshold increase, where X is an amount equal to our Big Bonus.

Silent

Creatures that try to detect you receive a 1d6 penalty on their detect roll. Your stealth rolls gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Big Bonus.

Dark Vision

You can see in the dark. Yeah, even magical darkness.

Detect Magic

Your Detect roll allows you to detect magic.

Carrier

Your encumbrance capacity is doubled.
