Basic Traits

Charming

Persuasion rolls gain a +1d6 bonus.

Wise

Insight rolls gain a +1d6 bonus.

Medic

Your rolls related to medical skills gain a +1d6 bonus.

Agile

Gymnastics rolls gain a +1d6 bonus.

Aware

Sense rolls gain a +1d6 bonus.

Strong

Muscle rolls gain a +1d6 bonus.

Tech Savvy

Advanced Tech rolls gain a +1d6 bonus.

Inspector

Search rolls gain a +1d6 bonus.

Fast

Your movement speed increases by 10.

Survivalist

Your ability to utilize survival skills in relevant situations gains a +1d6 bonus.

Silent

Your Sneak rolls gain a +1d6 bonus.

Dark Vision

You can see in the dark. Yes, even magical darkness.

Detect Magic

Your ability to detect magical auras or items in your vicinity is enhanced. Consider this a specialized use of the Insight or Search skills, depending on context.

Carrier

You gain an additional Item Slot.

Fast Reloader

Reloading is done instantaneously and doesn't use up any of your actions.

Technophile

Advanced Tech rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, showcasing your proficiency with cutting-edge technology and complex gadgets.

Escapist

Disarm and Lockpick rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, reflecting your adeptness at escaping binds and unlocking secrets.

Acrobat

Gymnastics rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, enhancing your ability to perform athletic feats and maintain balance in challenging situations.

Empath

Insight rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, enabling a deeper understanding of others' intentions and emotions.

Dominating Presence

Intimidate rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, making you more imposing in social confrontations and able to cow your enemies into submission.

Powerlifter

Muscle rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, reflecting superior physical strength and the ability to perform feats of brute force.

Influencer

Persuasion rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, indicating your talent for swaying others to your viewpoint through charm and eloquence.

Detective

Search rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, aiding in your ability to find hidden objects or clues that others might miss.

Perceptive

Sense rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, highlighting your keen senses and ability to pick up on subtle cues in your environment.

Con Artist

Sleight of Hand rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, making you adept at trickery, pickpocketing, and other forms of manual deception.

Shadow

Sneak rolls gain a +1d6 bonus, ensuring you move silently and unseen, whether you're infiltrating enemy strongholds or avoiding detection.

Night Owl

You can see in low light as if it were bright light, allowing you to move and act without penalty in darkness or dim conditions.

Quick Hands

You may perform one additional Sleight of Hand or Lockpick action as a free action per encounter.

Enduring

You gain a +1d6 bonus to any rolls for resisting fatigue, toxins, or harsh weather, demonstrating your rugged endurance.
