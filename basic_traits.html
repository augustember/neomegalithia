Basic Traits

Persuasive

Social rolls that attempt to use persuasion gain a +1d6 bonus.

Intimidating

Social rolls that try to use intimidation gain a +1d6 bonus.

Deceptive

Social rolls that try to use deception gain a +1d6 bonus.

Agile

Athletics rolls that use gymnastics gain a +1d6 bonus.

Musclehead

Athletics rolls that use brute muscle gain a +1d6 bonus.

Aware

Sense rolls gain a +1d6 bonus.

Computer Nerd

Tech rolls which use computers gain a +1d6 bonus.

Locksmith

Tech rolls which attempt to pick locks gain a +1d6 bonus.

Trap Breaker

Tech rolls which attempt to disarms traps gain a +1d6 bonus.

Inspector

Search rolls which attempt to investigate gain a +1d6 bonus.

Tracker

Search rolls which attempt to track a target gain a +1d6 bonus.

Observant

Sense rolls which attempt to visually scan an area gain a +1d6 bonus.

Active Listener

Sense rolls which attempt listen to something gain a +1d6 bonus.

Empathic

Insight rolls gain a +1d6 bonus.

Deft Hands

Stealth rolls that attempt to use sleight of hand gain a +1d6 bonus.

Shadow

Stealth rolls which attempt to sneak gain a +1d6 bonus.

Quick Hands

You may perform one additional Sleight of Hand or Lockpick action as a free action per encounter.

Night Owl

You can see in low light as if it were bright light, allowing you to move and act without penalty in darkness or dim conditions.

Dark Vision

You can see in the dark. Yes, even magical darkness.

Carrier

You gain an additional Item Slot.

Fast Reloader

Reloading is done instantaneously and doesn't use up any of your actions.
