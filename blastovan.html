Blasto Van

A van that blasts.

Lv 1 Vehicle,Size: 10 Foot Cube, Speed: 400 Feet, Max Occupancy 4

Enemies cannot target occupants of this vehicle.

HP 40, Energy 5, Defense 5
STR +5/15, AGL +5/15, WIS +5/15
Tech +5/15, Charm +0/10, Detect +5/15, Inspect +5/15, Medicine +5/15, Knowledge +5/15, Stealth +0/10

Blast Cannon

Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 0
Blasto Van Chooses a 10 foot cube of space within 100 feet of it. Any creatures within that space are targets of this ability. It rolls a +5 attack rolls against all targets' Evasion Threshold. On a hit, it deals 10/20 damage to the target, and that target is dazed.
