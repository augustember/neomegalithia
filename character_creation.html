Character Creation for Level 1

1. Choose a Name

It can be whatever you like.

2. Starting Level

You begin at level 1.

3. Starting Dice Allocation

You receive an initial set of dice to assign across skills, abilities, and defense rolls: 2 d12s, 3 d10s, 4 d8s, 5 d6s. Unassigned slots default to d4.

You might want to be care with how you assign these, since you are going to be assigning these across all of your skills, defense rolls, and abilities. You are absolutely capable of creating a very imbalanced character, such as one who hits hard with her abilities but is absolutely trash at performing skills, etc.

4. Defense Rolls Assignment

Assign dice to Defense Rolls (Toughness, Evasion, Willpower) to protect against attacks and hazards. Each has 1 die slot.

5. Skill Dice Allocation

Assign dice to skills for overcoming challenges. Each skill, including Athletics, Insight, Knowledge, and others, has 1 die slot.

6. Starting Experience

Your experience points begin at 0 at level 1.

7. Choose a Playable Creature

Select from creatures like Faun, Minotaur, and Merfolk, each offering unique traits and abilities. This choice affects your starting stats and abilities.

Playable Creatures

8. Action Allocation

At level 1, you receive 2 standard actions and 1 extra action.

9. Equipment Selection

Select your items and equipment from the starting equipment section. You have several slots which you may use to equip your various pieces of gear.

Choose one of the following to gain:
  • One big weapon.
  • One medium weapon and one shield.
  • One medium weapon and two small weapons.
  • Two medium weapons.

    • You gain the following:

  • One relic of your choice.
  • One torso armor of your choice.
  • Two field items of your choice.
  • Two single-use items of your choice.
