Creature Size

In terms of size, creatures are described by the cube size they occupy:

  • 5 Foot Cube: Standard size for most playable species.
  • 10 Foot Cube: Larger creatures that take up more space.
  • 2.5 Foot Cube: Smaller creatures with less spatial presence.
Not that most creatures literally exactly conform to cubic dimensions, but when creatures are described with these cubic dimensions, they are considered to occupy that approximate amount of space.

Occupying Space

  • A 5 Foot Cube creature can't share its cube with another of equal or larger size.
  • Smaller creatures can coexist in the same cube depending on their size.
