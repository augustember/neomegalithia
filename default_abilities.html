Default Abilities

Every character has the potential to defend themselves, regardless of their experience with formal combat training. This section has abilities accessible to all.

Basic Weapon Strike

Type: Utility, Attack

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Choose a target within this weapon’s range. Make an ability vs target’s Agility Threshold. On a hit, this deals the Weapon’s damage.

Block

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

When an ability hits you, you may negate an amount of damage equal to your this ability's Core Bonus.

Dodge

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

As you are being attacked, increase your Agility Threshold by an amount equal to the Core Bonus assigned to this ability.

Mentally Fortify

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

As you are being attacked, increase your Mind Threshold by an amount equal to the Core Bonus assigned to this ability.

Physically Fortify

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

As you are being attacked, increase your Strength Threshold by an amount equal to the Core Bonus assigned to this ability.

Disengage

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Decisive

You may move 5 feet away from a creature without triggering his Opportunity Attack.

Opportunity Attack

Type: Weapon, Attack

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

Target a creature that has either moved out an area directly adjacent to you or moves past you during his move action. Make an ability vs target’s Agility Threshold roll. On a hit, you deal the weapon’s damage.

Help

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

As an ally within your reach is performing an action, you may grant that ally an Advantage Bonus equal to this ability's Core Bonus.

First Aid

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 1

Action Type: Extra, Decisive

Choose a target creature in an adjacent space to yours. That creature has a number of HP restored equal to this ability's Core Bonus.

Delay Standard Action

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

During Fast Times, this ability allows you to preemptively use one of your held Standard Actions immediately before another combatant takes their action.
