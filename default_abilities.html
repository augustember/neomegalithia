Default Abilities

Every character has the potential to defend themselves, regardless of their experience with formal combat training. This section has abilities accessible to all.

Basic Weapon Strike

Type: Utility, Attack

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

This ability uses your Big Bonus. Choose a target within this weapon’s range. Make an ability vs target’s Agility Threshold. On a hit, this deals the Weapon’s damage.

Help

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

As an ally within your reach is performing an action, you may grant +1d6 to that ally's roll.

First Aid

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 1

Action Type: Extra, Decisive

Choose a target creature in an adjacent space to yours. That creature has a number of HP restored equal to your Medium Bonus.
