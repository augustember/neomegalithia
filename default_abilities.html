Default Abilities

Every character has the potential to defend themselves, regardless of their experience with formal combat training. This section has abilities accessible to all.

Basic Weapon Strike

Type: Utility, Attack

Dice Slots: 1

Dice Cost: 0

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Choose a target within this weapon’s range. Make an ability vs target’s Agility Threshold. On a hit, this deals the Weapon’s damage.

Help

Type: Support

Dice Slots: 1

Dice Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

As an ally within your reach is performing an action, you may grant bonus die to that ally's roll, where the size of that die is the same as the one for this ability.

First Aid

Type: Support

Dice Slots: 1

Dice Cost: 1

Action Type: Extra, Decisive

Choose a target creature in an adjacent space to yours. That creature has a number of HP restored equal this ability's die.
