Default Abilities

Every character has the potential to defend themselves, regardless of their experience with formal combat training. This section has abilities accessible to all.

Basic Weapon Strike

Type: Utility, Attack

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Choose a target within this weapon’s range. Make an ability vs target’s Agility Threshold. On a hit, this deals the Weapon’s damage.

Block

Type: Utility, Attack

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

When an ability hits you, you may negate an amount of damage equal to your Big Bonus.

Opportunity Attack

Type: Weapon, Attack

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Extra, Response

Target a crature that has either moved out an area directly adjacent to you or moves past you you during his move action. Make an ability vs target’s Agility Threshold roll. On a hit, you deal the weapon’s damage.
