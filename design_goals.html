Design Goals

This section is mostly for me to remember what my aims for this project are.

So, some stuff:

  • Keep things relatively simple and elegant.
  • Try to avoid mechanics that are cumbersome for players.
  • Find ways around stacking.
  • Be careful with exceptions to X rule.
  • Player autonomy is incredibly important.
  • Game is designed with casuals in mind, but has enough depth under the hood that grognards can enjoy it... probably.
