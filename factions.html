Factions

The Ecosynths

They believe that all creatures have a place in the universe, and believe in prosperity and balance for all. They dwell in hightech structures which aim to peacefully integrate into natural surroundings.

Gaia Nation

They believe high technology was what once brought about the destruction of previous civilizations, so they tend to have a very technophobic ideology. They tend to dwell and work within earth berms and adobe buildings.

The Enders

This is settlement of people consisting of mostly fae and merfolk who deeply believe that to exist is to suffer, and therefore all sentient life must end. So they seek various means of ending all life in the Universe.

Disciples of The Weaver

Worshippers of entity known as The Weaver. The Disciples tend to succumb to madness and are generally extremely hostile.

The Successors

A faction devoted to remembering and carrying on the legacy of humanity. They tend to have small settlements within human ruins, such as collapsed skyscrapers.
