Neomegalithia TTRPG

By E.G.

In the world of Terra Megalithia, humanity is nothing but a memory...

Welcome to Neomegalith TTRPG, players take on roles within the Adventurer's Guild of Terra Megalithia, a world where human civilizations are extinct, and fauns, minotaurs, and merfolk dwell in futuristic societies that have been integrated into their natural surroundings. Gameplay revolves around exploring overgrown ruins, engaging in combat, and navigating the politics and dangers of a world filled with magic and ruins of the its predecessors. The game uses a classless system, and damage of abilities are not rolled separately from rolling to hit. An energy point system (it's basically MP) is used for special abilities, and it has card-based initiative system.
