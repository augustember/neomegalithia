Welcome to Neomegalithia TTRPG!

What You Need to Play

To embark on your journey through Megalithia, you will need:

  • This Rulebook: This guide to the world, mechanics, and most things you need to know to play the game (probably).
  • Character Sheets: For creating and tracking your character’s progress, abilities, inventory, and more.
  • Cards: (like index cards) for the Action Card mechanic.
  • Dice: Terra Megalithia uses six-sided dice, which are referred to as d6s.
  • Pencils and Erasers: To change your character sheet as your character develops.
  • Note-taking Materials: For jotting down important plot points, clues, and personal reminders about the game world and your adventures.
  • Imagination: The key to bringing the world of Terra Megalithia to life, imagining your character’s actions, and immersing yourself in the story.
  • Friends (or Tolerable Acquaintances): Megalithia is designed for a group of players, typically including one Game Master (GM) who narrates and referees the adventure and players who take on the roles of characters within the story.

Optional but recommended:

  • Battle Mat and Miniatures: For visually tracking movement and positioning during combat scenarios.
  • Thematic Music and Props: To enhance the atmosphere and immersion of your gaming sessions.

Characters in Megalithia

Characters are the heart of the story, adventurers and heroes navigating a world of ancient secrets and burgeoning civilizations. Creating a character involves choosing a race, each with unique abilities and affinities that defines their skills and approach to challenges. Characters grow by mastering their abilities, acquiring gear, and making choices that shape their journey. In this world, characters must balance personal ambition with the needs of their communities, facing threats both mundane and supernatural. Success in Megalithia requires cleverness, bravery, and teamwork, as the world's mysteries unfold around the characters, inviting them to leave their mark on its history.

Characters have Core Bonuses, health points, energy points, defense points, turn cards, abilities, and skills which will be explained later.
