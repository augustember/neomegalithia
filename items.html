Single-Use Items

Using and interacting with any of these is an extra action.

Item Effects Encumbrance
Good Potion Restores your current HP by an amount equal to half its maximum. 1
Great Potion Restores your current HP by a value equal to your maximum. 1
Antidote Cures poison for a target. 1
Coffee Restores of your current EP by a value equal to half its maximum. 1
Espresso Full restores your current EP to maximum. 1
