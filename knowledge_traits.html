Knowledge Traits

Magic Knowledge

Knowledge Rolls that pertain to magic gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Medium Bonus.

Local Knowledge

Rolls that pertain to geographical features, landmarks, local laws, customs, and regional history gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Medium Bonus.

Dungeoneering Knowledge

Rolls related to navigating dungeons, identifying traps, understanding the construction and decay of underground structures, and the ecology of subterranean creatures gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Medium Bonus.

Cultural Knowledge

Knowledge Rolls that pertain to history or knowledge about various nations and cities gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Medium Bonus.

Nature Knowledge

Rolls related to identifying natural phenomena, weather patterns, plants, animals, and survival strategies gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Medium Bonus.

Creature Knowledge

Rolls related to identifying information concerning various creatures of the world gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Medium Bonus.
