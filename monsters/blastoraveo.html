Blasto Raveo

Arm Cannon Fae.

Lv 2 Fae Creature, Experience Granted: 50, Size: 5 Foot Cube, Speed: 25 Feet

HP 40, Defense 2
Standard Actions 2, Extra Actions 1
Toughness 11, Evasion 16, Willpower 10
Tech 16, Charm 11, Detect 16, Inspect 17, Medicine 10, Knowledge 11, Stealth 11

Blaster Attack


Type: Attack Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Targets one creature within 50 ft. The target makes an Evasion roll vs 16. On a failure, this deals 5/10 plasma damage to the target.

Master Blast


Type: Attack Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Targets one creature within 50 ft. The target makes an Evasion roll vs 16. On a failure it deals 10/15 plasma damage and the target is slowed (the target loses 1 standard action and her movement speed is halved until the end of Fast Times). Blasto Raveo takes 1 magical toxicity damage.
