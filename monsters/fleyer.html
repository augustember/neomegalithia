Fleyer

Thiefy Fae.

Lv 2 Fae Creature, Experience Granted: 30, Size: Medium, Speed: Ground 30 Feet, Flying 30 Feet

Immune to fire, ice, water, plasma, and earth. Flies.

HP 20, Energy 10, Defense 0
Standard Actions 1, Extra Actions 1
Strength 11, Evasion 13, Willpower 14
Tech 11, Charm 11, Detect 19, Inspect 14, Medicine 10, Knowledge 11, Stealth 11

Ram

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Fleyer moves to space next to a creature target within 30 feet of itself. That target makes an Evasion vs 13 roll. On a failure, the target receives 5/10 damage.

Plasma Grenade

Type: Magical, Attack Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Fleyer chooses a 10 cubic space within 30 feet of itself. All targets within that space make an Evasion vs 15 roll. Every creature that failed this roll takes 4/8 plasma damage.

Sleep

Type: Magical, Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 2
The Fleyer targets a creature within 30 feet. The target makes a Willpower vs 10 roll. On a failure, the target asleep.
