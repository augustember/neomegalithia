Fleyer

Thiefy Fae.

Lv 2 Fae Creature, Experience Granted: 30, Size: 5 Foot Cube, Speed: Ground 30 Feet, Flying 30 Feet

Immune to fire, ice, water, plasma, and earth. Flies.

HP 20, Energy 10, Defense 0
Standard Actions 1, Extra Actions 1
STR +1/11, AGL +3/13, WIS +4/14
Tech +1/11, Charm +1/11, Detect +9/19, Inspect +4/14, Medicine +0/10, Knowledge +1/11, Stealth +1/11

Ram

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Fleyer Guy moves to space next to a target within 30 feet of itself. Fleyer makes a +3 roll against that target's Agility Threshold. On a hit, the target receives 5/10 damage.

Plasma Grenade

Type: Magical, Attack Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Fleyer Guy chooses a 10 cubic space within 30 feet of itself. Fleyer makes a +5 roll against the Agility Thresholds of all creatures within that space. On every hit, Fleyer deals 4/8 plasma damage.

Sleep

Type: Magical, Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 2
Fleyer chooses a target within 30 feet of it. Fleyer makes a +6 roll against against the Target's Current HP. On a hit, that target falls asleep.
