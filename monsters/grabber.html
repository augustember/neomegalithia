Grabber

Grapply Fae

Lv 4 Fae Creature, Experience Granted: 100, Size: 5 Foot Cube, Speed: 30 Feet

Immune blunt damage. Cannot be grappled.

HP 110, Energy 10, Defense 3
Standard Actions 2, Extra Actions 1
STR +6/16, AGL +4/14, WIS +2/12
Tech +4/14, Charm +2/12, Detect +6/16, Inspect +6/16, Medicine +0/10, Knowledge +1/11, Stealth +0/10

Grapple

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Grabber chooses a creature target within 5 feet of itself. Grabber makes a +7 attack roll against the target's Strength or Agility Threshold. On a hit, that target is grappled by Grabber, and the target's speed is 0 until the target is no longer grappled. As a Standard Action, the target may make an Agility or Strength roll against Grabber's Toughness Threshold. If that roll hits, then the target is no longer grappled.

Suplex

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Grabber may only do this if he is already grappling a target. Grabber chooses a target he is presently grappling. Grabber rolls a +8 against that target's Agility or Strength Threshold. On a hit, that target take 7/14 blunt damage.
