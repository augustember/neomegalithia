Red Moutho

This dude is oddly stealthy for how big he is...

Lv 2 Creature, Experience Granted: 10, Size: 2 Foot Cube, Speed: 10 Feet

Immune to sharp. Weak to fire. Amorphous.

HP 66, Energy 10, Defense 3
Standard Actions 2, Extra Actions 1
Toughness 13, Evasion 16, Willpower 11
Tech 10, Charm 13, Detect 16, Inspect 11, Stealth 16

Slam Dance

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 0
Targets one creature within 10 feet of itself. The targets makes an Evasion vs 18 roll. On a failure, the target takes 5/10 blunt damage.

Bye Bye Bite

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Targets one creature within 5 feet of itself. The targets makes an Evasion vs 18 roll. On a failure, it deals 8/16 sharp damage. On a hit, the target is now poisoned.

Hecka Dodge

Type: Support
Action Types: Extra, Response
Energy Cost: 1
If something attacks Red Moutho and misses, Red Mouth may use this ability to move 10 feet.
