Red Moutho

This dude is oddly stealthy for how big he is...

Lv 1 Creature, Experience Granted: 10, Size: 2 Foot Cube, Speed: 10 Feet

Immune to sharp. Weak to fire. Amorphous.

HP 66, Energy 10, Defense 3
Standard Actions 2, Extra Actions 1
STR +3/13, AGL +6/16, WIS +1/11
Tech +0/10, Charm +3/13, Detect +6/16, Inspect +1/11, Medicine --, Knowledge --, Stealth +6/16

Slam Dance

Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 0
Targets one creature within 10 ft, rolling +8 against the target's Agility Threshold. On a hit, it deals 5/10 blunt damage.

Bye Bye Bite

Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Targets one creature within 5 feet of itself. Makes a +8 vs target’s Agility Threshold roll against that target. On a hit, it deals 7/14 sharp damage. On a hit, the target is now poisoned.

Hecka Dodge

Action Types: Extra, Response
Energy Cost: 1
If something attacks Red Moutho and misses, Red Mouth may use this ability to move 10 feet.
