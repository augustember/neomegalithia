Spiky Heady

He's spiky!.

Lv 1 Fae Creature, Experience Granted: 25, Size: Medium, Speed: 30 Feet

HP 25, Energy 8, Defense 1
Standard Actions 2, Extra Actions 1
Toughness 15, Evasion 13, Willpower 12
Tech 10, Charm 10, Detect 15, Inspect 10, Knowledge 12, Stealth 13

Melee Attack

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 0
It chooses a target within 5 feet of itself. The target makes an Evasion vs 16 roll. On a failure, the target takes 3/6 blunt damage.

Heavy Attack

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
It chooses a target within 5 feet of itself. The target makes an Evasion vs 16 roll. On a failure, the target takes 10/15 blunt damage and the target is dazed.
