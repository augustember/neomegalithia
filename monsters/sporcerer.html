Sporcerer

This dude lives in dank places and stuff.

Lv 2 Fae Creature, Experience Granted: 10, Size: 2 Foot Cube, Speed: 10 Feet

Immune to sharp. Weak to fire. Amorphous.

HP 35, Energy 10, Defense 0
Standard Actions 2, Extra Actions 1
Toughness 10, Evasion 13, Willpower 15
Tech 15, Charm 10, Detect 15, Inspect 10, Medicine 15, Knowledge 15, Stealth 13

Spore Pollen Attack

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Sporcerer chooses any number of creature targets within 30 feet of itself. All targets make a Toughness vs 17 roll. Every target that failed takes 4/6 poison damage.

Death Cap

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Targets one creature within 10 feet of itself. The target makes a Toughness vs 17 roll. On a failure, the target takes 5/10 poison damage (poison damage temporarily decreases your Hit Point Maximum by an amount equal to your how much Poison Damage you have been dealt).

Telespore

Type: Support
Action Type: Extra, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Allows Sporcerer to move up to 20 feet away to an unoccupied space it can see. Response abilities such as Opportunity Attack do not trigger for this movement and if Sporcerer was restrained, it is no longer.
