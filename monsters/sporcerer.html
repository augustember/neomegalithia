Sporcerer

This dude lives in dank places and stuff.

Lv 2 Fae Creature, Experience Granted: 10, Size: 2 Foot Cube, Speed: 10 Feet

Immune to sharp. Weak to fire. Amorphous.

HP 35, Energy 10, Defense 0
Standard Actions 2, Extra Actions 1
STR +0/10, AGL +3/13, WIS +5/15
Tech +5/15, Charm +0/10, Detect +5/15, Inspect +0/10, Medicine +5/15, Knowledge +5/15, Stealth +3/13

Spore Pollen Attack

Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Sporcerer makes a +7 roll against all targets of its choosing within a 30 foot range. For every hit, those targets take 4/6 poison damage.

Death Cap

Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Targets one creature within 10 feet of itself. Makes a +7 vs target’s Strength Threshold roll against that target. On a hit, it deals 5/10 poison damage and poisons the target.
Telespore Action Type: Extra, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Allows Sporcerer to move up to 20 feet away to an unoccupied space it can see. Response abilities such as Opportunity Attack do not trigger for this movement and if Sporcerer was restrained, it is no longer.
