Thiefy Guy

Thiefy Fae.

Lv 3 Fae Creature, Experience Granted: 50, Size: 5 Foot Cube, Speed: 50 Feet

Immune to grappling. If Thiefy Guy steals something, then Thiefy Guy tries to run as best as he can.

HP 50, Energy 10, Defense 0
Standard Actions 2, Extra Actions 1
STR +2/12, AGL +5/15, WIS +3/13
Tech +5/15, Charm +2/12, Detect +5/15, Inspect +5/15, Medicine +2/12, Knowledge +1/11, Stealth +10/20

Steal

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Thiefy Guy chooses a target within 5 feet of it. Thiefy Guy makes a +6 roll against the target's Agility Threshold. On a hit, Thiefy Guy steals a random item from the target.

Thiefy Kick

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 0
Thiefy Guy chooses a target within 5 feet of him. Thiefy Guy makes a +6 roll against that target's Agility Threshold. On a hit, Thiefy Guy deals 6/12 blunt damage to the target.
