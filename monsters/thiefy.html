Thiefy Guy

Thiefy Fae.

Lv 3 Fae Creature, Experience Granted: 50, Size: 5 Foot Cube, Speed: 50 Feet

Immune to grappling. If Thiefy Guy steals something, then Thiefy Guy tries to run as best as he can.

HP 50, Energy 10, Defense 0
Standard Actions 2, Extra Actions 1
Toughness 12, Evasion 15, Willpower +3/13
Tech 15, Charm 12, Detect 15, Inspect 15, Medicine 12, Knowledge 11, Stealth 20

Steal

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 1
Thiefy Guy chooses a target within 5 feet of it. The target makes an Evasion vs 16 roll. On a failure, Thiefy guy steals a random item from the target.

Thiefy Kick

Type: Attack
Action Type: Standard, Decisive
Energy Cost: 0
Thiefy Guy chooses a target within 5 feet of him. The target makes an Evasion vs 16 roll. On a failure, the target takes 6/12 blunt damage.
