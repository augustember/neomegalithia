Novice Abilities

These abilities require an Ability Rank of novice or above to gain.

Lethal Strike

Type: Weapon, Attack

Energy Cost: 1

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Choose a single target within reach. Make an attack roll vs target’s Agility Threshold. On a hit, hit deals the weapon’s damage plus an amount equal to your this ability's Core Bonus.

Fly

Type: Support

Energy Cost: 1

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Grants you the ability to fly for 1 hour. Your vertical movement has the same speed as your ground movement.

Type: Magical, Support

Energy Cost: 1

You turn invisible until Fast Times end you gain an Advantage Bonus to your Agility Threshold equal to this ability's Core Bonus.

Energy Cost: 1

Penalizing Strike

Type: Weapon, Attack

Energy Cost: 1

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Choose a target within 5 feet of you. Make an ability roll vs that target’s Agility Threshold. On a hit, you deal the weapon’s damage to this target and that target takes a 1d6 penalty to their next attack roll. This ability does not stack with itself.

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Choose a target within this weapon’s reach. Make an ability roll vs target’s Agility Threshold against that target. If successful, this ability deals the weapon’s damage plus an amount of damage equal to your Highest Bonus.

Lesser Clone

Type: Magical, Control

Energy Cost: 2

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

This ability conjures a single clone of the caster, capable of performing simple tasks and actions independently. Unlike mere illusions, this clone possesses a fraction of the caster's physical capabilities and can interact with the physical world, though its Strength and resilience are significantly reduced compared to the original. You may only have as many clones in existence equal to your ability rank (1 for novice, 2, for expert, and 3 for master).

This clone inherits the caster's physique. It carries mock versions of all physical appearance and basic knowledge.The clone is created within 5 feet of the caster and lasts until Fast Times end or until it takes any form of damage, at which point it poofs out of existence.

It can perform actions you could, with some limitations: It has 1 HP, it cannot use abilities or actions that require energy, it lacks bonuses or thresholds in any of its skills, hits against it are automatic (require no roll), has mock versions of items which you were equipped or carrying which lack any benefit those things had, and has no Ability or Skill Ranks or Bonuses.

It can make a melee attack roll (its ability vs target’s Agility Threshold) against a creature within 5 feet of it, where it is using a melee clone weapon that deals 2/4 blunt damage. You may decide to dismiss this clone (poof it out of existence) anytime as an extra action on or outside of your turn.

Telekinetic Grab or Push

Type: Utility, Attack (Sometimes)

Energy Cost: 1

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Choose a target object or creature as a target. If it’s a creature, make an ability vs the target’s Strength or Wisdom Threshold. On a hit (not applicable for an object) propel a single object or creature within 40 feet in a direction of up to 30 feet of its initial position, dealing 5/10 damage to any creature which has collided with a creature or object involved with this ability.

Telekinetic Disarm

Type: Utility, Attack

Energy Cost: 1

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Target an opponent's held item or equipment within 30 feet, attempting to wrench it from their grasp and bring it to you or drop it to the ground. You make an ability roll vs Target’s Wisdom or Strength Threshold. On a hit, the target item flies away from them to a position within 10 feet of the creature holding that target item. This does not work items fixated to the creature’s body, such as cybernetic parts which are a part of their anatomy.
