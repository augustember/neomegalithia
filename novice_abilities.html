Lesser Clone

Type: Magical, Control

Die Slot: Yes, Consumable

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

This ability conjures a single clone of the caster, capable of performing simple tasks and actions independently. Unlike mere illusions, this clone possesses a fraction of the caster's physical capabilities and can interact with the physical world, though its Strength and resilience are significantly reduced compared to the original. Using this ability gives you 1 magic toxicity damage. You may only have as many clones in existence equal to your ability rank (1 for novice, 2, for expert, and 3 for master).

This clone inherits the caster's physique. It carries mock versions of all physical appearance and basic knowledge.The clone is created within 5 feet of the caster and lasts until Fast Times end or until it takes any form of damage, at which point it poofs out of existence.

It can perform actions you could, with some limitations: It has 1 HP, it cannot use abilities or actions that require energy, it lacks bonuses or thresholds in any of its skills, hits against it are automatic (require no roll), has mock versions of items which you were equipped or carrying which lack any benefit those things had, and has no Ability or Skill Ranks or Bonuses.

It can make a melee attack roll vs target’s Evasion against a creature within 5 feet of it, where it is using a melee clone weapon that deals 2/4 blunt damage. You may decide to dismiss this clone (poof it out of existence) anytime as an extra action on or outside of your turn.

Telekinetic Grab or Push

Type: Utility, Attack (Sometimes)

Die Slot: Yes, Consumable

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Choose a target object or creature as a target. If it’s a creature, make an ability vs the target’s Toughness or Willpower. On a hit (not applicable for an object) propel a single object or creature within 40 feet in a direction of up to 30 feet of its initial position, dealing 5/10 damage to any creature which has collided with a creature or object involved with this ability.

Telekinetic Disarm

Type: Utility, Attack

Die Slot: Yes, Consumable

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Target an opponent's held item or equipment within 30 feet, attempting to wrench it from their grasp and bring it to you or drop it to the ground. Roll vs Target’s Willpower or Toughness (your choice). On a hit, the target item flies away from them to a position within 10 feet of the creature holding that target item. This does not work items fixated to the creature’s body, such as cybernetic parts which are a part of their anatomy.
