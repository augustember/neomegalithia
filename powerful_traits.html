Powerful Traits

Blood Magic

You may spend your HP as if it were energy. For every 1 energy that would be spent, it expends 5 HP.

Hexenhaft Potency

Your attacks ignore the resistance of creatures. It does not ignore a creature’s immunities, however.

Tough

Your Strength Threshold increases by an amount equal to your Big Bonus. This scales with any increases of your Medium Bonus.

Painful

Your abilities deal extra damage equal to the value of your Big Bonus.

Magi Energy

This increases your Energy Maximum by an amount equal to your Big Bonus.This scales with any increases to your Big Bonus.

Snake Eyes

When you roll when two or more dice would come up as 1, you may reroll those dice.

Resolute

Your Wisdom Threshold increases by an amount equal to your Big Bonus. This scales with any increases of your Medium Bonus.

Elemental Affinity

Choose 1 damage type among fire, ice, earth, and air. Attacks with your chosen type gain an Advantage Bonus equal to your Medium Bonus. You are resistant to damage of your chosen damage type.

Healthy

You are granted an additional HP total which is your Big Bonus x 10. This scales with any increases of your Big Bonus.

Potent Healing

When you give an effect that restores HP, you restore an additional HP equal to your Big Bonus and all of your healing effects subtract one wound from any targets you are healing.

Sniper

The range of your ranged attacks double.

Iron Constitution

You are unaffected by effects that would poison (the condition), set you aflame, freeze, petrify, or curse you. This does not negate poison damage.

Iron Hide

You gain an additional value to your Natural Defense equal to your Medium Bonus.

Hurt Locker

Creatures that are grappled by you gain a temporary decrease equal to your Big Bonus equal for whichever Threshold they are using against any grapple attempt you make.

Weapon Mastery

Choose a weapon type. You now get an Advantage Bonus for rolls with that weapon equal to your Big Core Bonus.

Martial’s Fury

Every additional ability used in a single round grants you a +1 Advantage Bonus to attack rolls for the rest of the round. When a new round begins, this Advantage Bonus goes back down to +0.

Immovable

You cannot be moved unwillingly by the effects or abilities.

Thorny Body

When a creature fails its attack roll within your range, that creature is dealt damage equal to your Medium Bonus.

Thorny Body

When a creature fails its attack roll within your range, that creature is dealt damage equal to your Medium Bonus.

Adaptive

During Fast Times, if you take a particular type of damage, you may decide to be resistant to that type of damage for the rest of the encounter. You may apply this to something else during the encounter, but if you do, it drops the resistance of the last one you chose.

Strong Arms

You can wield big weapons as if they were small ones.

Four Arms

You gain two arms and hands. Your hand slots increase by +2 and your strap slots increase by +1

Energetic

You gain +1 Standard Action. This may taken 2 times: Once after attaining level 3 and once after attaining level 8

Lightfoot

You may run on water and vertical surfaces. If you end your turn on those you will fall, however.

Spider Climb

Enables movement up, down, and across vertical surfaces and ceilings at walking speed without checks. Hands remain free. Does not protect against falls when damaged or on magically slippery surfaces.

Sneaky Damage

If you make a damaging attack roll against a target that is unaware of you, you deal twice the damage you normally would with that attack.

Evasive

Your Agility Threshold increases by an amount equal to your Big Bonus. This scales with any increases of your Medium Bonus.

Quick to Act

You can perform Default Abilities as if they were Extra Actions regardless of their action type.
