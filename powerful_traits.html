Powerful Traits

Blood Magic

You may spend your HP as if it consumable dice. For every size of dice you would use this way, you take that size's worth of damage.

Hexenhaft Potency

Your attacks ignore the resistance of creatures. It does not ignore a creature’s immunities, however.

Tough

Your Toughness rolls gain a +1 helper die.

Resolute

Your Willpower rolls rolls gain a +1 helper die.

Evasive

Your Agility rolls gain a +1 helper die.

Painful

Your abilities deal extra damage equal to the size of your helper die.

Magi Energy

This increases your Energy Maximum by an amount equal to your helper die. When your helper die increases, you gain that many more.

Elemental Affinity

Choose 1 damage type among fire, ice, and plasma. Attacks with your chosen type gain +1 to your chosen damage type. You are resistant to damage of your chosen damage type.

Healthy

You are granted an additional HP total which is your Big Bonus x 10. This scales with any increases of your Big Bonus.

Potent Healing

When you give an effect that restores HP, you restore an additional amount equal to the size of your helper die.

Sniper

The range of your ranged attacks doubles.

Iron Constitution

You are unaffected by the effect of all active damage chips. If you have more damage than you have health for, you'll still die, though.

Iron Hide

Your Natural Defense increases by 2.

Hurt Locker

When you grapple a creature, it takes 1 hurt locker damage. As long as the creature has at least 1 active hurt locker damage, this creature's ability thresholds are halved.

Weapon Mastery

Choose a weapon type. Your rolls with that weapon gain a dX bonus, where X is the size of your helper die.

Martial’s Fury

Each additional ability used in a single round grants you a bonus to attack rolls for the rest of the round, where the bonus starts as d6 and increases to d8, and d8 to d10, and d10 to d12. This bonus resets to +0 when a new round begins.

Immovable

You cannot be moved unwillingly by effects or abilities.

Thorny Body

When a creature fails its attack roll within your range, that creature takes damage equal to the size of your helper die.

Adaptive

During Fast Times, if you take a particular type of damage, you may decide to be resistant to that type of damage for the rest of the encounter. You may apply this to a different type of damage during the encounter, but if you do, the resistance to the previous damage type is lost.

Strong Arms

You can wield big weapons as if they were small ones, negating the usual penalties or requirements for their use.

Four Arms

You gain two additional arms and hands, increasing your Hand Slots by +2.

Energetic

You gain +1 Standard Action. This benefit can be taken twice: once after reaching level 3 and once after reaching level 8.

Lightfoot

You may run on water and vertical surfaces. If you end your turn on these surfaces, you will fall, however.

Spider Climb

Enables movement up, down, and across vertical surfaces and ceilings at walking speed without checks. Hands remain free. Does not protect against falls when damaged or on magically slippery surfaces.

Sneaky Damage

If you make a damaging attack roll against a target that is unaware of you, you deal twice the damage you normally would with that attack.

Quick to Act

You can perform Default Abilities as if they were Extra Actions, regardless of their usual action type.
