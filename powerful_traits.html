Powerful Traits

Blood Magic

You may spend your HP as if it were energy. For every 1 energy that would be spent, it expends 10 HP.

Hexenhaft Potency

Your attacks ignore the resistance of creatures. It does not ignore a creature’s immunities, however.

Tough

Your Strength rolls gain a +1d6. This scales with any increases of your Medium Bonus.

Resolute

Your Willpower rolls gain a +1d6. This scales with any increases of your Medium Bonus.

Evasive

Your Agility rolls gain a bonus of +1d6. This scales with any increases of your Medium Bonus.

Painful

Your abilities deal extra damage equal to +1d6.

Magi Energy

This increases your Energy Maximum by an amount equal to +1d6. This scales with any increases to your Big Bonus.

Elemental Affinity

Choose 1 damage type among fire, ice, earth, and air. Attacks with your chosen type gain a +1d6 to damage rolls. You are resistant to damage of your chosen damage type.

Healthy

You are granted an additional HP total which is your Big Bonus x 10. This scales with any increases of your Big Bonus.

Potent Healing

When you give an effect that restores HP, you restore an additional amount equal to +1d6, and all of your healing effects subtract one wound from any targets you are healing.

Sniper

The range of your ranged attacks doubles.

Iron Constitution

You are unaffected by effects that would poison (the condition), set you aflame, freeze, petrify, or curse you. This does not negate poison damage.

Iron Hide

Your Natural Defense gains a bonus of +1d6. This scales with your Medium Bonus.

Hurt Locker

Creatures that are grappled by you suffer a penalty to their Threshold rolls equal to -1d6 for any grapple attempt you make against them.

Weapon Mastery

Choose a weapon type. Your rolls with that weapon gain a +1d6 bonus.

Martial’s Fury

Each additional ability used in a single round grants you a +1 bonus to attack rolls for the rest of the round. This bonus resets to +0 when a new round begins.

Immovable

You cannot be moved unwillingly by effects or abilities.

Thorny Body

When a creature fails its attack roll within your range, that creature takes damage equal to +1d6.

Adaptive

During Fast Times, if you take a particular type of damage, you may decide to be resistant to that type of damage for the rest of the encounter. You may apply this to a different type of damage during the encounter, but if you do, the resistance to the previous damage type is lost.

Strong Arms

You can wield big weapons as if they were small ones, negating the usual penalties or requirements for their use.

Four Arms

You gain two additional arms and hands, increasing your Hand Slots by +2.

Energetic

You gain +1 Standard Action. This benefit can be taken twice: once after reaching level 3 and once after reaching level 8.

Lightfoot

You may run on water and vertical surfaces. If you end your turn on these surfaces, you will fall, however.

Spider Climb

Enables movement up, down, and across vertical surfaces and ceilings at walking speed without checks. Hands remain free. Does not protect against falls when damaged or on magically slippery surfaces.

Sneaky Damage

If you make a damaging attack roll against a target that is unaware of you, you deal twice the damage you normally would with that attack.

Quick to Act

You can perform Default Abilities as if they were Extra Actions, regardless of their usual action type.
