Rankless Abilities

These abilities have no training rank in order to be gained. Unlike Default Abilities, these are not abilities that all characters have. These must be chosen upon being made to gain new abilities.

Eavesdrop

Type: Utility

Energy Cost: 1

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Your hearing amplifies distant sounds or conversations, allowing the caster to listen in without being detected.

Lesser Telekinesis

Type: Utility

Energy Cost: 1

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Grants the caster the ability to manipulate and move objects from a distance without physical contact. This spell can target objects that weigh up to 10 pounds within a 30-foot radius. The caster can lift, move, or manipulate the object within the spell's range, such as opening an unlocked door, retrieving an item from a shelf, or subtly moving an object to distract someone. The control is precise enough to perform simple tasks, like pouring a potion or writing a brief message with a pen, but not for more complex manipulations requiring fine motor skills. The effect lasts until Fast Times ends.

Modulate

Type: Utility

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Allows the caster to adjust the intensity of a light source or sound within 30 feet, making it brighter, dimmer, louder, or quieter at will. This ability has no energy cost but requires line of sight.

Detect Magic

Type: Utility

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Grants the caster the ability to sense magical effects within a 60-foot radius until Fast Times ends or 20 minutes passes, whichever comes first. This mainly allows you to know if an effect is magical, but doesn't inform you any other specifics about that thing.

Know Direction

Type: Utility

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

A simple spell that informs the caster of their cardinal orientation, making it impossible to get lost under natural conditions.

Stage Magic

Type: Utility

Energy Cost: 0

Action Type: Standard, Decisive

Allows you to weave minor magical effects for amusement, utility, or distraction. This ability taps into the user's innate magical energy to produce subtle but noticeable effects without significant energy expenditure. This ability can:

  • Briefly change the color, taste, or temperature of small objects.
  • Produce harmless sensory effects like a puff of smoke, a soft jingle, or a flash of light.
  • Create small, temporary illusions that can fit in the palm of their hand, lasting a few moments.
  • Levitate or animate small, lightweight objects for simple tasks such as self-tidying or delivering messages across short distances.

    • Lesser Telepathy

    Type: Utility

    Energy Cost: 0

    Action Type: Extra, Decisive

    Establishes a mental link with one willing creature within sight until Fast Times ends or 20 minutes, whichever happens first, allowing silent communication over any distance as long as both parties remain on the same plane of existence.

    Lesser Unlock

    Type: Utility

    Energy Cost: 1

    Action Type: Standard, Decisive

    This automatically unlocks locks with a difficulty of Medium or below.

    Push

    Type: Magical, Control

    Energy Cost: 1

    Action Type: Standard, Decisive

    Emits a burst of force from the caster's palm, capable of pushing an object or creature directly 5 feet away.

    Transmute Weapon

    Type: Magical, Control

    Energy Cost: 1

    Action Type: Extra, Decisive

    You transform the damage property of your weapon to from one type to another: sharp, blunt, fire, ice, air, earth, light, dark, and corrosive). This effect lasts until Fast Times ends and does not stack with itself.

    Summon Rations

    Type: Magical, Utility

    Energy Cost: 1

    Action Type: Extra, Decisive

    You summon a food ration on an unoccupied space of your choosing within 30 feet. This food ration is a single-used item which restores an amount equal to your Big Bonus points upon being eaten. Eating this ration is an Extra Action.

    Taunt

    Type: Control

    Energy Cost: 1

    Action Type: Extra, Decisive

    Choose a target within 30 feet of you. Make an ability roll vs that target’s Wisdom Threshold. On a hit, that target prioritizes attacking you until Fast Times ends.

    Understand Language

    Type: Utility

    Energy Cost: 0

    Action Type: Extra, Decisive

    You understand a language you hadn’t before for the next hour. This allows you to speak, read, and write in the chosen language.

    Lesser Disarm Trap

    Type: Utilityl

    Energy Cost: 1

    Action Type: Extra, Decisive

    You automatically disarm a trap of Medium difficulty or below.

    Somewhat Evasive

    Type: Utility

    Energy Cost: 1

    Action Type: Extra, Decisive

    Until Fast Times ends you gain a temporary increase to your Agility Threshold equal to your Small Bonus.

    Somewhat Stealthy

    Type: Utility

    Energy Cost: 1

    Action Type: Extra, Decisive

    For the next minute you gain an Advantage Bonus and temporary increase for your Stealth Skill equal to your Small Bonus.
