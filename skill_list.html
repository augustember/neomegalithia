List of Skills

Athletics

Encompasses physical activities that require strength, speed, or endurance. This includes running, jumping, and climbing, generally measured against a physical obstacle's Difficulty Threshold.

Insight

Relates to understanding others' intentions, emotions, and truthfulness, often in social interactions or when assessing someone's honesty.

Knowledge

Covers academic, practical, and specialized information across a variety of fields and subjects, reflecting a character's education and study.

Performance

Includes artistic expression and entertainment skills such as music, acting, dance, and other performance arts.

Search

Focuses on the ability to find information, objects, or people through careful observation or investigation.

Sense

Encompasses perceptual abilities like seeing, hearing, and smelling, particularly useful in detecting nuances or changes in the environment.

Social

Deals with interpersonal interactions, including negotiation, persuasion, intimidation, and deception. Specific actions may be contested by targets' various thresholds such as Gullibility, Obstinacy, or Resolve.

Stealth

Covers skills related to moving unseen and unheard, useful for avoiding detection. Success is often measured against an observer's ability to notice such actions.

Tech

Involves expertise with technological devices and systems, from computers to complex machinery, including lockpicking and disarming traps. Challenges typically face a Difficulty Threshold based on the complexity of the task.
