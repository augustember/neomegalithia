List of Skills

Strength

As a Bonus

Strength covers actions requiring brute physical power like lifting heavy weights, breaking barriers, or engaging in physical contests. It reflects your character's muscular power and capacity for forceful actions.

As a Threshold

It signifies your character's ability to endure physical challenges, resist being moved or knocked over, and their overall endurance against fatigue and physical strain.

Agility

As a Bonus

Agility encompasses skills requiring finesse, quick reflexes, and coordination, such as dodging attacks, performing acrobatic maneuvers, or sneaking past guards. It's a measure of your character's speed, balance, and precision.

As a Threshold

It represents your character's capability to evade or resist effects targeting their mobility or dexterity, including avoiding traps, sidestepping hazards, and maintaining balance in treacherous conditions.

Wisdom

As a Bonus

Wisdom reflects insight, judgment, and intuition, useful for discerning intentions, resisting mental manipulation, or understanding complex situations. It covers your character's perceptiveness, spirituality, and attunement to their surroundings.

As a Threshold

It indicates your character's resistance against mental and psychological effects, such as deception, persuasion, or magical illusions. It represents mental fortitude and the ability to remain composed under stress.

Tech

As a Bonus

Tech involves knowledge and skill with technology and complex mechanisms. This includes picking a lock, disarming a device, hacking devices, repairing or sabotaging equipment, and understanding technological systems.

As a Threshold

It represents your character's ability to counter technological threats or challenges, such as disarming traps, deciphering complex locks, or resisting electronic warfare tactics.

Detect

As a Bonus

Detect covers your character's ability to notice hidden details, uncover secrets, or spot concealed threats. It encompasses perception, investigation, and awareness.

As a Threshold

It signifies your character's aptitude for avoiding being surprised or caught off-guard, their ability to spot ambushes, and their skill in observing subtle cues in their environment.

Inspect

As a Bonus

Inspect encompasses your character's ability to closely examine objects, environments, or magical auras to gather information, detect hidden compartments, understand intricate mechanisms, or discern the quality and authenticity of items. It's helpful for uncovering clues, deciphering puzzles, and noticing details that others might overlook.

As a Threshold

It represents your character's skill in avoiding deception through visual or magical means, such as identifying illusions, spotting forgeries, or noticing tampering with objects. It indicates the character's proficiency in scrutinizing their surroundings to avoid traps and ambushes, ensuring they're not easily misled by appearances.

Charm

As a Bonus

Charm represents your character's ability to influence others through persuasion, deception, or leadership. It includes negotiating, lying, entertaining, and seducing.

As a Threshold

It reflects your character's resistance to social manipulation and their ability to maintain composure and assertiveness in social interactions, resisting charms, threats, and persuasion.

Knowledge

As a Bonus

Knowledge encompasses your character's academic learning, wisdom, and understanding of various subjects, from history and lore to science and magic.

As a Threshold

It represents your character's ability to recall relevant information under pressure, apply theoretical knowledge to practical situations, and resist being misled by false information.

Medicine

As a Bonus

Medicine covers your character's skill in healing wounds, diagnosing illnesses, and treating poisons. It includes first aid, surgery, and knowledge of medicinal herbs and treatments.

As a Threshold: It signifies your character's proficiency in applying medical care under challenging conditions, stabilizing critical injuries, and countering effects that target physical health.

Stealth

As a Bonus

Stealth includes your character's ability to move silently, hide from view, and avoid detection. It's crucial for sneaking past enemies, ambushing targets, and moving unseen.

As a Threshold

It represents your character's skill in remaining hidden when being searched for, minimizing noise or signs of their presence, and evading detection mechanisms like traps or alarms.

Survival

As a Bonus

Survival encompasses your character's ability to endure harsh environments, find food and water, navigate unfamiliar terrain, and track creatures. It reflects adaptability, outdoor skills, and resourcefulness.

As a Threshold

It indicates your character's resilience against the elements, their ability to avoid natural hazards, and their skill in staying oriented and finding resources in the wild.
