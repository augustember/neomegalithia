List of Skills

Stealth

Covers the skills related to moving unseen and unheard, including hiding and silent movement. Stealth rolls typically roll against a creature's Detect Treshold.

Athletics

Encompasses physical activities requiring strength, speed, or endurance, such as running, jumping, and climbing. Athletics rolls are generally against a Difficulty Threshold when being performed against an obstacle.

Detect.

Focuses on perceptual abilities, such as seeing, hearing, and smelling, especially in detecting changes or nuances in the environment. Also covers the ability to find information, objects, or people, including investigation and tracking. When trying to find a stealthy creature, you roll against that creature's Stealth Threshold.

Insight

Relates to understanding the intentions, emotions, and truthfulness of others. This is generally done in response to a Persuasion Deception Threshold of a creature.

Knowledge

Encompasses academic, practical, and specialized information across various fields and subjects.

Tech

Involves understanding and manipulating technological devices and systems, including computers, lockpicking, disarming traps, gadgets, and complex machinery. Tech rolls are generally rolled against a Difficulty Threshold involving these things.

Social

Deals with interactions with others, including negotiation, persuasion, intimidation, deception, and leadership. The thresholds of creatures this skill rolls against depends on the nature of what you're trying to do. Attempts to deceive roll against a creature's Gullibility Threshold, attempts to persuade roll against a creature's Obstinacy Threshold, and attempts to intimidate roll against a creature's Resolve Threshold.

Perform

Includes skills related to artistic expression and entertainment, such as music, acting, and dance.
