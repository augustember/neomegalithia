Unusual Status Conditions

Poisoned

At the beginning of every turn lose 1 HP and take a 1d6 penalty on dice rolls. This condition is permanent until this condition is removed either by an ability, item, or other effect.

Stunned

All of your actions for the round are now put at the end of the initiative order (your next actions are skipped until the end of the round). If more than one creature would become stunned in a single round, the last to be stunned goes in the back of the initiative order. Your action cards remain this way until the end of Fast Times.

Weakened

All of your actions for the round are now put at the end of the initiative order (your next actions are skipped until the end of the round). If more than one creature would become stunned in a single round, the last to be stunned goes in the back of the initiative order. Your action cards remain this way until the end of Fast Times.

Dazed

For the remainder of the round and the next you get a 1d6 penalty for your rolls.

Slowed

Your movement speed is halved. You lose 1 Standard Action. Duration: 1 minute.

Frozen

Your movement speed is 0 and your ability and skill rolls get a 1d6 penalty for the next minute. If you would take fire damage, you are no longer frozen.

Silenced

Magical abilities cannot be performed for 1 minute.

Restrained

Your movement speed is 0.

Frightened

Before making an action, you must make a Wisdom roll vs an 18 difficulty. If you fail, you may not perform that action. And all action costs for this ability get used up as if you had done them.

Broken Limb

All rolls requiring that limb gain a 1d6 penalty. If this is a leg, your movement is reduced by half. If this is an arm, you may not wield equipment with that arm.

Aflame

You are on fire. At the start of every round the fire makes a +0 attack roll vs your Toughness Threshold. If it hits, it deals 4/8 damage to you. You are no longer aflame if you are attacked with water or ice damage.
