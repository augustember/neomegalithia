Upgrading a Character

Like many other RPGs, this uses experience points which grant certain levels upon gaining a certain amount of experience points.

Levels and Experience

Levels start at 1 and can be a maximum of 12. A particular level grants you specific benefits, such as Core Bonus increases, new traits, Skill Upgrades, Ability Rank Increases, and new abilities. Experience Points are numbers which determine when you gain levels upon meeting a certain threshold. Various encounters reward experience points.

Benefits Upon Leveling Up

Every level you gain +10 HP Maximum and +1 Energy Maximum plus all the stuff contained within the experience chart below.

Core Bonus Increases

Various Core Bonuses increase by +1 at certain levels. The Big Bonus increase by +1 every level, the Medium Bonus increases by +1 every even level, and the Small Bonus increases by +1 every level which is a multiple of 3.

Ability Rank Increases

Your Ability Rank applies across all of your gained abilities, and increases at certain levels. For each upgrade for this it goes from whichever one it is to the next, where the ranks go from Novice, Expert, and Master. Whenever you gain a new rank, you may choose 3 abilities for the rank which was increased, where you assign your Big, Medium, and Small Bonuses among them of your choosing.

Ability Bonus Upgrades

When you receive an Ability Upgrade at certain levels, you may increase the Core Bonus type of 2 of your abilities. The ability you increase goes from none to small, small to medium, or medium to big. You may not upgrade an ability which is already big.

Skill Upgrades

Upon gaining certain levels, you can enhance your skills, just like you upgrade your abilities.

When you reach certain levels, you get to improve 1 skill that hasn't yet received a Big Bonus. This means you can upgrade its Core Bonus from none or small to medium, or from medium to big.

Standard Actions Increase

A standard Actions Increase increases how many standard actions you may use per turn by +1.

New Traits

Traits are passive effects which you always have upon taking one. Every time you are made to take New Traits, you gain 1 of each trait type (Knowledge, Basic, and Powerful) of your choosing listed in the Traits section.

Skill Upgrades

Skills represent specialized areas of expertise, such as Stealth, Craft, or Knowledge.

At certain levels you may increase the bonus type of one your skills that doesn’t already have a Big Bonus.
