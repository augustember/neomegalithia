Character Upgrades

As characters gain experience and level up, they unlock new abilities, dice allocations, skills, and traits, enhancing their journey through the game.

Levels and Experience

Characters progress from level 1 up to level 13, with each level bringing specific advancements and perks. The progression is determined by experience points earned through game play.

Benefits Upon Leveling Up

In addition to increasing HP Maximum by +10 at every level and players receive enhancements according to the following experience chart:

Experience required for leveling follows a polynomial growth pattern, with the amount needed increasing as characters reach higher levels.

Gaining New Abilities

Ability Rank Upgrade

As a level 1 you have that ability rank of Novice. However, at levels 4 and 8 your ability rank goes up. Your ability rank determines what abilities you may acquire and assign dice to. For each upgrade for this it goes from whichever one it is to the next, where the ranks go from Novice, Expert, and Master. Whenever you gain a new rank, you may choose 3 abilities for the rank which was increased, and you may assign a die to an ability's die slot if it has one.

Assigning New Dice

Every level you gain an new type of die as seen on the experience chart. If an ability doesn't have any die assigned to it, you may assign one of your core dice to it as long as as you have that ability. If you have already assigned a die to it previously, you may switch out a die with a lower size with a new one if a bigger size and put that die which was replaced in some other skill or ability's die slot of your choosing. You can do the same for skills, except you are considered to have all of the skills.

New Traits

At levels 5 and 9 you gain new traits. Traits are passive effects which you always have upon taking one. Every time you are made to take New Traits, you gain 1 of each trait type (Knowledge, Basic, and Powerful) of your choosing listed in the Traits section.
